About Me

  • I am a Ph.D. student at Tianjin University of Technology, advised by Prof. Zan Gao.
  • My research centers on trustworthy multimedia intelligence, with a focus on visual forensics, AIGC safety, large multimodal model-generated media forensics, and embodied AI safety.
  • I have published or accepted papers at AAAI, ACM Multimedia, and IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology.
  • I previously collaborated with the Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Shandong Academy of Sciences on intelligent media analysis and visual perception.

Research Directions

Visual Forensics and Manipulation Localization

Pixel-level detection and localization of manipulated, spliced, and inharmonious image regions under complex real-world editing traces.

AIGC and Large Multimodal Model-Generated Media Forensics

Detection, attribution, and explanation of forged media produced or edited by generative models and large multimodal systems.

Embodied AI Safety

Security and reliability of perception-centric embodied agents, especially under forged visual observations, distribution shifts, and adversarial scene evidence.

Interpretable and Generalizable Forensic Learning

Multi-scale inconsistency modeling, cross-dataset generalization, and failure-case diagnosis for trustworthy forensic decision making.
